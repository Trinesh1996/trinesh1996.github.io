About

Growing up in the Mother City, South Africa, diverse in all its beauty and culture, the most fundamental aspect in life you learn is that of community and community-building. I feel that its something thats needed in todays globally connected world. I have always been curious by nature, having developed an aptitude for Music at an early age, I realised that the universality of melodies, the art of expressing yourself through this medium can change the world
I started off with Piano and guitar, then violin and drums. Other ideas i've spent a considerable time thinking about is the questions of life and the nature of reality and consciousness. All this idea pondering led me to Artifiical Intelligence.

The Wikipedia Definition is:

"Artificial intelligence (AI, also machine intelligence, MI) is intelligence demonstrated by machines, in contrast to the natural intelligence (NI) displayed by humans and other animals. In computer science AI research
is defined as the study of "intelligent agents": any device that perceives its environment and takes actions that maximize its chance of successfully achieving its goals."

At the moment i'm studying AI. My goals are to continue to learn and work on some of the gloabal problems i'm in intersted in - Transportation and Ecological Sustaibility.
